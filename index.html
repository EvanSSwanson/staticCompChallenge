About

The Flames were founded during the NHL's expansion era in the 70's. Originating in Atlanta, Georgia, the Flames were not very successful during their time in the Peach State. This lack of success, plus an overall lack of pre-existing hockey culture in the South, led the Flames to garner little support from the city. Thus, in 1980, the team relocated to hockey-crazy Calgary, Alberta.
